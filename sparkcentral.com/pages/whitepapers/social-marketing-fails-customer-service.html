The Achilles Heel: Why Social Marketing Fails at Customer Service

About whitepaper

Think your marketing department should respond to customers on social media? After all, they’re already on social media, so why not answer a few questions while they’re at it, right?

You wouldn’t have marketing answer phones in a call center, so why would they manage customer inquiries on social media?

They shouldn’t.

What you will learn

  • Define clear departmental roles so marketing and customer service don’t collide on social media
  • Maximize your social customer service so that CS enhances your marketing efforts while remaining separate
  • Increase customer loyalty and satisfaction by having a dedicated team respond to social customers, answer their questions, solve their problems and engage in conversations
