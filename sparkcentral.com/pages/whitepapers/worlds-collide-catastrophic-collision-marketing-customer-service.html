Worlds Collide: The Catastrophic Collision Between Marketing and Customer Service

About Whitepaper

Are your marketing department and customer service department battling over control of your social media? Are customers getting overlooked, lost or buried in the confusion?

A collision between marketing and customer service could be costing your company valuable time and resources. Social marketing platforms aren’t designed for customer service and neither are marketers.

To maximize your CS team’s efforts, you need a dedicated Social CS platform that will allow agents to address customer issues in real time and provide every customer with highly personalized support.

What You Will Learn

  • Understand your social customers: what frustrates them and what motivates them to buy
  • Define departmental roles to avoid a collision between marketing and customer service
  • Create an effective social customer service strategy to ensure every customer receives personalized attention in real-time
  • Increase your profits by decreasing response times
