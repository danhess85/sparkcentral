It's 2014, and NEWSFLASH: customers are demanding service at practically the speed of light. Whether it's waiting for a barista to craft a coffee, or tweeting a question about weather delays, customers expect "freaky fast" service. If you're like most people, you're rarely in the mood to call customer service while at the airport. With Sparkcentral, the Brussels Airlines social team embraces and even pioneers the current speedy demands of customers by quickly responding to their busy travelers.
